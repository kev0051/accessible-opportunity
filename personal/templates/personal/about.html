About Our Website

Our website is designed to help people with disabilities find jobs and research opportunities. It provides a platform for employers to browse profiles of potential employees and for users to network by making posts, sending messages, and updating their profiles and documents.

We are committed to making our website accessible to everyone and have implemented a range of accessibility features to ensure that all users can use our site with ease.
